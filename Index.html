About Us

We provide services for individuals who wish for Hajj or Umrah to be performed on behalf of their dead relatives or those who are too old and weak to perform the pilgrimage personally, to pay to get the pilgrimage done on their behalf. We also provide services such as flight bookings, hotel bookings, and personalized tour guidance for people who wish to explore the two holy cities of Makkah and Medinah and the entirety of The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Visit historic sites such as Badr, Uhud, The Cave of Hira, and many more.

Our Services

  • Hajj on your behalf: Reap the rewards of having Hajj or Umrah perfomed on your behalf.
  • Flight and Hotel bookings: Relax and have us take charge of your smooth transition to and stay in The Kingdom.
  • Personalized Tour Guidance: Allow us to provide you the full thrill of experiencing all the The Kingdom has to offer.
  • Consultation: Advice and planning for your spiritual journey.
